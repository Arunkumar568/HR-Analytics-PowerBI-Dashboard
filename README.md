# Project Overview

This HR Analytics Dashboard was developed using Power BI to analyze workforce demographics, employee attrition, salary trends, and departmental performance.

The dashboard enables HR teams and business leaders to identify employee turnover patterns and make data-driven workforce decisions.

---

# Business Problem

Employee attrition directly impacts productivity, recruitment costs, and organizational performance.

The objective of this dashboard is to identify:

- Employee attrition trends
- High-risk departments
- Salary-based turnover patterns
- Workforce demographics
- Employee retention opportunities

# Analysis Performed

## Attrition by Department

Research & Development recorded the highest employee attrition, followed by Sales.

## Attrition by Age Group

Employees aged 26–35 showed the highest turnover.

## Attrition by Salary Slab

Employees earning less than 5K experienced significantly higher attrition compared to higher salary groups.

## Attrition by Job Role

The dashboard identifies job roles with elevated employee turnover rates.

## Attrition by Education

Attrition trends were analyzed across educational backgrounds.

---

# Key Insights

- 16.17% workforce attrition observed.
- Research & Development department showed highest attrition.
- Employees aged 26–35 represented the largest attrition segment.
- Lower salary groups experienced significantly higher turnover.
- Attrition patterns vary across departments and job roles.
